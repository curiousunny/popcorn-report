## What we want to know

*Are children of different racial, ethnic, and social backgrounds experiencing different quality of life after illness?*

EXPAND (EXamining associations between Pediatric quality of life and sociodemographic chAracteristics of childreN in CanaDa) will estimate whether children's race and ethnicity are linked to differences in quality of life — including physical, social, emotional, and school functioning, as well as mental health outcomes like anxiety and depression.

## How we're doing it

*The work is being guided by POPCORN's equity and patient engagement teams.*

Analyses will be guided by POPCORN's Equity, Diversity, Inclusion + Indigeneity team and our Patient Engagement team, so the work is shaped by the communities it's meant to serve.

## Where we are now

*Preparing the ethics application and finalizing sample size calculations.*

We are working with the Data Pillar to confirm sample sizes for the analyses, and we are submitting a Research Ethics Board application for the project.
